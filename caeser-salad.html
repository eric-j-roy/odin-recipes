Caeser Salad

Description

Looking for a fresh, healthy appetizer or side? Here is a quick and easy Caesar recipe!

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Mayonnaise
  • Anchovy paste
  • Fresh lemon juice/li>
  • Fresh minced garlic
  • Dijon or whole grain mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk all dressing ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Store in fridge until ready to use.
  3. In a large bowl, mix all of the lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. When ready to serve, mix dressing in with the rest of the salad.
