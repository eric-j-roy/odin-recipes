Fettuccine Alfredo

Here's a tasty Italian dish that can be made with minimal effort! With only 5 ingredients, this recipe is quick, easy, and won't require a ton of dishes to clean!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a large pot, add salt to the water to season the pasta. Once boiling, add pasta and cook according to package instructions.
  2. Saute the garlic and butter together until softened.
  3. Add heavy cream and let simmer over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes to thicken. Add salt and pepper as well as some Parmesan cheese. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Save some pasta water to help thin out the sauce as needed.
  5. Toss alfredo sauce with fettuccine pasta and add some parmesan cheese. Top of with some more parmesan cheese after mixing.
  6. Garnish with Italian parsley if desired.
