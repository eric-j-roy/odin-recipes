Christmas Sugar Cookies

Description

There's no better way to get into the holiday spirit than baking some warm Christmas sugar cookies! This easy to follow recipe is perfect for anyone looking for a tasty holiday treat!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted butter, softened
  • 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Red and green sanding sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add the butter, cream cheese, sugar, eggs, vanilla, and almond extract. Using an electric mixer, beat the ingredients together until light and fluffy, about 4 to 5 minutes.
  2. Using a rubber spatula, scrape down the sides of the bowl. Add the flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt. Turn the hand mixer to low speed and combine the ingredients just until a soft dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  4. Once the dough has been chilled for 2 hours, remove it from the refrigerator and preheat the oven to 350F.
  5. Use a 1-inch cookie scoop to portion the dough, and use your hands to roll dough into 1-inch balls. Place the dough balls on the parchment paper-lined cookie sheet, at least 1 inch apart.
  6. Using a fork, make a crisscross pattern pressing down lightly into the cookie dough.
  7. Sprinkle the top of each cookie with green or red sugar.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 9 to 11 minutes. Be sure to not overcook these cookies. They should not brown on the edges at all. You want them to be soft, and they should look a bit undercooked when you pull them out of the oven.
  9. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to continue cooling.
